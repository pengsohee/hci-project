HISTORY

SaffCoZ was born from the passion of Safiya Colenze, a young woman from a small coastal town surrounded by lavender fields and citrus orchards. Inspired by the natural scents of her homeland, Safiya began experimenting with blending oils and fragrances in her family's workshop. Her first creation, a perfume named "Gratitude," captured the essence of her hometown and quickly became a local favorite.
